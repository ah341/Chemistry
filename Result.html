Conclusion


The experiment confirmed the hypothesis: adding different substances to a solution does change its pH level, depending on whether the substance is acidic or basic. Acidic substances, such as Coca Cola or lemon juice, lowered the pH, making the solution more acidic. Basic substances, such as baking chlorine or soap, raised the pH, making the solution more basic.
